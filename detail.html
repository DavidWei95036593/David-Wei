UCLA has a large population of Asians,however, it does not mean the people who is not Asian can not fit in. Students know how to balance their school life and social life, and they are always friendly to freshman or transfer students. Most students creates their own clubs (UCLA has over 1000 clubs), kick-start businesses, run organizations and are deeply involved with the community. In general, 45% of the students live in campus, while 55% live off campus. Although student at each grade live in the same house, foreign students are required to live in a unique house and stay there to help other foreign students who might not be in the same grade level. Its fans fill the stands for some of the most storied teams in NCAA history.
